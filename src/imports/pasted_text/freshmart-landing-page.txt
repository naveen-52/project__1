Create a pixel-perfect, high-fidelity, production-ready Figma landing page for an Online Supermarket Shopping Platform called "FreshMart".

Business Overview:

FreshMart allows customers to browse grocery products online, add items to cart, place orders, and receive home delivery. Every order is reviewed and confirmed by the supermarket owner before delivery to ensure product availability, order accuracy, and customer satisfaction.

Design Goal:

Create a premium, modern, trustworthy grocery delivery platform that looks comparable to Blinkit, Zepto, Instamart, BigBasket, and Instacart. The design should feel like a real startup ready for launch, not a wireframe, template, student project, or generic AI-generated design.

Visual Style:

* Clean and modern UI
* Premium ecommerce design
* White background
* Primary Green: #22C55E
* Secondary Orange: #F97316
* Dark Text: #111827
* Light Background: #F9FAFB
* Modern typography (Inter or Poppins)
* Rounded corners (16px–24px)
* Soft shadows
* Large whitespace
* Modern illustrations
* Real grocery product imagery
* Friendly and trustworthy appearance

Layout Requirements:

* Desktop width: 1440px
* Use a professional 12-column grid
* Use an 8px spacing system
* Use Auto Layout for every component
* Fully responsive design
* Consistent visual hierarchy
* Developer-friendly structure

Typography Hierarchy:

* H1: 56px Bold
* H2: 40px Bold
* H3: 28px Semi-Bold
* Body: 18px Regular
* Small Text: 14px
